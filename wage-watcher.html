Wage Watcher

See your earnings grow live, on your wrist.

Wage Watcher is a simple real-time wage tracker that lets you watch your earnings increase as you work.

Real-Time Earnings

Watch your income update live throughout the day based on your pay rate and working hours.

Built for Apple Watch

Infuencial People

Curious how your earnings compare globally? Explore how your income stacks up alongside some of the world’s highest earners and wealthiest individuals, a fascinating way to put time and money into perspective.

Simple Setup

Enter your wage once using the companion app on iPhone and let Wage Watcher handle the rest.

Why Wage Watcher?

  • Stay motivated during long shifts
  • Understand the value of your time
  • See progress happen in real time
  • Gain a new perspective on earnings worldwide
