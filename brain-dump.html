Brain Dump

Capture now. Organise later. Free your mind.

Brain Dump is a simple note-tracking app designed for how thoughts actually happen, quickly, unpredictably, and often when you’re busy doing something else.

Instead of trying to organise everything perfectly in the moment, Brain Dump lets you drop ideas, reminders, tasks, and random thoughts instantly throughout the day. Then, when you have time in the evening, you can return and sort everything calmly and clearly.

Capture Thoughts Instantly

Got an idea? A task you don’t want to forget? Just dump it into the app and move on. No folders or decisions required in the moment.

Organise When You’re Ready

Review and sort your notes later when your mind is calm and focused. Brain Dump separates capturing from organising, the way your brain naturally works.

Powerful Search

Quickly find past thoughts, ideas, or reminders with fast, simple search so nothing gets lost.

Designed for Mental Clarity

Brain Dump helps reduce cognitive overload by giving your thoughts a reliable place to live outside your head.

Why Brain Dump?

  • Capture ideas without interruption
  • Reduce mental clutter
  • Stay focused during the day
  • Organise on your own schedule
  • Never lose a good idea again

Brain Dump isn’t about perfect productivity systems.
It’s about getting thoughts out of your head so you can focus on what matters now.
